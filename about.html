Legacy of Excellence

Anchorage is the flagship technical symposium of the Department of Ship Technology (DOST), CUSAT. Established to bridge the gap between academic theory and maritime industry standards, DOST has been pioneering Naval Architecture education in India for decades.

Anchorage 2026 continues this tradition by blending cutting-edge technology with the raw spirit of maritime exploration. From workshops to flagship competitions, every event is designed to challenge minds, spark innovation, and celebrate the ocean engineers of tomorrow.

HISTORY LOG

Our Journey

2015 — GENESIS
Anchorage was born as a small departmental fest, bringing together naval architecture students for the first time under one technical symposium.
2018 — EXPANSION
Anchorage grew to a national-level event, attracting participants from IITs, NITs, and other premier maritime institutions across India.
2022 — DIGITAL EVOLUTION
Post-pandemic, Anchorage embraced hybrid formats, introducing online workshops and digital competitions while retaining its signature hands-on experience.
2026 — NEW HORIZONS
Anchorage 2026 sets sail with the most ambitious programme yet — expanded events, industry partnerships, a Pro Show, and an Indian Navy Expo.
