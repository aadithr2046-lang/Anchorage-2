Legacy of Excellence

Anchorage is the flagship technical symposium of the Department of Ship Technology (DOST), CUSAT. Established to bridge the gap between academic theory and maritime industry standards, DOST has been pioneering Naval Architecture education in India for decades. Anchorage 2026 continues this tradition by blending cutting-edge technology with the raw spirit of maritime exploration.
